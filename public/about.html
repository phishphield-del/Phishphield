🔐 About PhishShield

PhishShield is designed to safeguard your digital activities by combining advanced phishing detection techniques with trusted security integrations. Our goal is to provide users with a reliable, real-time defense against online scams and malicious attacks.

⚙️ Key Security Features:

  • 🌐 Google API Integration – We use Google's Safe Browsing API and trusted sources like PhishTank to identify and block dangerous websites instantly.
  • 💾 Secure Database Storage – All data is stored in a protected database with strong encryption and regular security audits, ensuring your information remains private.
  • 🧠 AI & Pattern Recognition – Smart algorithms analyze suspicious keywords, URLs, and domain patterns to detect even the most deceptive phishing attempts.
  • 🚀 Real-Time Threat Alerts – Users are immediately notified if a scanned URL poses a security risk, allowing quick and safe decisions.

With PhishShield, you’re not just scanning URLs—you’re staying one step ahead of cybercriminals. Our mission is to create a safer digital world where your data and identity remain secure.
